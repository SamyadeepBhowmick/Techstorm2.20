About The Event

B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology presents you Techstorm 2.20 where we aim at diversifying the manifold fields of technology.

With the advent of 2nd decade of the 21st century(2011), B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology kickstarted its annual Tech-Fest, "Techstorm". It has since been recognized as one of the most celebrated Tech-Fests of the city, with each edition out-doing its predecessor and making its own legacy to be kept. As an engineering institute, BPPIMT celebrates Techstorm as a signature event and has been pushing for innovation and excellence throughout 10 years of its existence.

Yes, Techstorm celebrates its 10th anniversary, a decade of attending to the zeal of thousands of students every year, this very year, in 2020. More institutes, excitement like never before, new events and a lot more! Needless to say, only an extravaganza justifies the mark of a decade, and an extravaganza we shall have.

While continuing to celebrate this legacy, BPPIMT welcomes you to the 10th edition of Techstorm - The Technical Festival of BPPIMT family. Join us on 7th, 8th and 9th April. We promise you it will be bigger and better than ever before.
