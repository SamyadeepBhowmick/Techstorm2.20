About The Event

B.P.PODDAR INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT & TECHNOLOGY presents to you ‘TECHSTORM 2.19’ where we aim at diversifying the manifold fields of technology . Ever since the advent of the techfest in our institute from the year 2011,we have always believed in the amulgumation of technology and imagination. “Imagination is more important than knowledge . For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire World, stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution. ” - Albert Einstein While continuing to celebrate this legacy, B.P.P.I.M.T welcomes you to this edition of ‘TECHSTORM 2.19’ - The Technical Festival of B.P.P.I.M.T family. We invite you all to come and unleash your technical skills and creativity, be our esteemed guests on the 15th and 16th of March, 2019 and take back along with you an enriching experience that you have never before witnessed .

Where

Downtown Conference Center, New York
